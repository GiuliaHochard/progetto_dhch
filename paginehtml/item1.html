Pagina 1 recto

The Things that never
can come back, are
several -
Childhood - some
forms of Hope - the Dead -
But Joys like men
may sometimes make a
Journey
And still abide -

We do not mourn
for Traveler or Sailor -
Their Routes are fair -
But think - enlarged -
of all that they will
tell us -
Returning here -

"Here"! There are
typic Heres -

Pagina 2 recto

Foretold Locations -
The Spirit does
not stand -
Himself - at what -
soever Fathom
His Native Land -
